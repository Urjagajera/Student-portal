Attendance Policy at CHARUSAT

Regular attendance is essential for academic success. Below is the attendance requirement by branch and general policy.

Minimum Requirement

Students must maintain at least 75% attendance in each subject to appear in exams.

Medical Leave

Medical certificates must be submitted within 3 days to be considered for attendance relaxation.

Subject-Wise Tracking

Attendance is tracked for each subject individually. Weekly reports are available via the student portal.
