About CHARUSAT

Charotar University of Science and Technology (CHARUSAT), located in Changa, Gujarat, is one of the most reputed universities in India known for academic excellence, research, innovation, and community engagement.

Our Vision

To become a world-class center of excellence in education and research, nurturing responsible citizens and innovative leaders who contribute to the progress of society.

Our Mission

  • Provide quality education in science, technology, and management.
  • Encourage interdisciplinary learning and research.
  • Promote innovation, entrepreneurship, and ethics.
  • Uplift rural and urban communities through technology and knowledge sharing.

Why Choose CHARUSAT?

CHARUSAT stands out because of its commitment to quality, student-centric approach, and industry relevance. Here's why students prefer CHARUSAT:

  • NAAC A+ Accredited University
  • Modern campus with advanced laboratories, Wi-Fi, and digital classrooms
  • Strong industry-academia linkages and internships
  • 100% placement assistance for eligible students
  • Clubs, hackathons, sports, cultural fests, and tech-events to enhance personality

Our Campus

The CHARUSAT campus is spread across lush green land in the education-friendly region of Anand District. With state-of-the-art infrastructure, an eco-friendly environment, and vibrant student life, the campus promotes holistic growth.

Institutes Under CHARUSAT

  • CSPIT – Chandubhai S. Patel Institute of Technology
  • DEPSTAR – Devang Patel Institute of Advance Technology and Research
  • MTIN – Manikaka Topawala Institute of Nursing
  • RPCP – Ramanbhai Patel College of Pharmacy
  • PDPICA – Indukaka Ipcowala Institute of Management
  • AIRIO – ARIBAS Institute of Life Sciences

Student Life at CHARUSAT

From vibrant fests like **Cognizance** and **Techfest**, to technical clubs like IEEE, CSI, and robotics groups, student life at CHARUSAT is active and full of opportunities to explore creativity, leadership, and teamwork.

Research and Innovation

CHARUSAT encourages research through funded projects, Ph.D. programs, and innovation labs. Students and faculty work together on solving real-world problems using modern technology and interdisciplinary knowledge.
