Engineering Branches at CHARUSAT

At CHARUSAT, each engineering branch nurtures creativity and technical skills. Here's a look at the different paths you can explore:

Computer Science and Engineering

Builds a strong foundation in programming, data science, algorithms, and problem-solving.

Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning

Focuses on AI models, neural networks, deep learning, and intelligent systems design.

Computer Engineering

Blends software and hardware development, system architecture, and embedded programming.

Information Technology

Deals with databases, web development, networking, and IT service management.

Electronics & Communication

Focuses on signal processing, IoT, robotics, and communication technologies.

Electrical Engineering

Includes power electronics, smart grids, electrical circuits, and automation systems.

Mechanical Engineering

Covers machine design, CAD, thermal systems, and manufacturing processes.

Civil Engineering

Specializes in structural engineering, geotechnics, and sustainable construction.
