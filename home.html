Our Institutes

CSPIT – Chandubhai S. Patel Institute of Technology

Established in 2000, CSPIT is the oldest and most prestigious institute under CHARUSAT. It offers B.Tech programs in Computer, IT, EC, Electrical, Mechanical, and Civil engineering. Known for industry ties, internships, and hands-on labs.

DEPSTAR – Devang Patel Institute of Advance Technology and Research

DEPSTAR offers futuristic programs like Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning, Data Science, and Cyber Security. With advanced infrastructure and research culture, it's a hub for next-gen tech education.

RPCP – Ramanbhai Patel College of Pharmacy

RPCP focuses on pharmacy education, pharmaceutical sciences, and research. It is AICTE and PCI approved, and ranked among top pharmacy colleges in Gujarat. Offers D.Pharm, B.Pharm, M.Pharm, and Ph.D. programs.

MTIN – Manikaka Topawala Institute of Nursing

MTIN offers B.Sc Nursing, M.Sc Nursing, and Ph.D. programs. It prepares students for hospital and clinical care, with dedicated labs, hospital training, and expert faculty.

PDPICA – Indukaka Ipcowala Institute of Management

PDPICA delivers MBA and BBA programs with a strong focus on leadership, entrepreneurship, and corporate management. It hosts regular industry interaction, internships, and case-based learning.

ARIP – Ashok & Rita Patel Institute of Physiotherapy

Offers BPT and MPT programs. Students undergo rigorous clinical training and internships, with access to advanced therapy labs, rehab centers, and patient-care programs.

ARIBAS – Ashok & Rita Institute of Biological & Applied Sciences

Offers B.Sc., M.Sc., and Ph.D. programs in Biotechnology, Microbiology, and other life sciences. ARIBAS is known for its research-oriented curriculum and bio-labs.
